Have you ever wanted to watch a movie - but wished you could overcome the language barrier?
Or what if a bunch of diverse, multilingual people wish to watch different dubbed versions of the same movie?

To sync multiple audio sources with a video source playing on a host machine is difficult and tedious to do manually. But Dubberwala is a unique app that provides this functionality and seamlessly syncs different audio sources with one another as well as with the video source, providing a truly personalised audio experience.

Say goodbye to arguments over which version to watch - now peacefully play separate audios for the same video, completely personalised and without distractions.
